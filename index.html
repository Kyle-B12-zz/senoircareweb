Meet Monica

I am a responsible, upbeat, and cheerful person who offers companionship, social interaction and peace of mind to families in need. I can provide transportation to run errands and assist with medical appointments. I have extensive experience with those who suffer from Dementia and Alzheimer’s Disease. I provide light housekeeping and pet care as well. I am a non-medial caregiver with excellent references, cover most of Otsego County. Please contact me for more information and rates!
